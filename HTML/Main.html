About the Camp

Here at UTSA, high school girls with high achieving academic levels are able to experience first hand experience on the STEM and engineering field. The girls are able to work with graduate students and professors who are knowledgable in their field to learn and apply their knowledge to local projects and research.

The campers are able to challenge themselves through rigorous coursework throughout the month of July, and learn to be familiar with multiple softwares that will be utilized in college and future careers.
